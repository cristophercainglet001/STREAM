What's New
        As part of our commitment to continuous improvement, this update further strengthens the STREAM System's compliance with the standards and guidelines of the Department of Education (DepEd).
Please be informed that reports and records generated by the STREAM System are designed to align with the applicable DepEd policies, guidelines, and issuances governing School Forms (SF1–SF10). 
        
        These enhancements help improve the accuracy, consistency, and integrity of school records while supporting more efficient data management.
To ensure accurate and reliable reports, all users are encouraged to review and verify encoded information before generating official school forms.

Thank you for your continued trust and support in using the STREAM System. Your feedback plays an important role in helping us enhance the system and provide better tools for teachers and school personnel.
